ARE YOU READY?

Before you start exercising. If you have spent a long time without stepping on a gym, we recommend that you go little by little, without forcing yourself. Do not try to do in a couple of days, everything you haven't done in years, that's why it's better to be prudent and move forward with the exercises over time. If, on the contrary, you are used to doing some exercises, but do not lift weights, do not think that this is going to be easy.
